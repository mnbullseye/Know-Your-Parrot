# Know-Your-Parrot
A webapp that has all the knowledge and stuff you need for your parrots. You can order things for your parrot and look up guides on how to take care of your parrot.

**#Project Theme: Avian Care & E-Commerce**
Our project, "Know Your Parrot," is a comprehensive resource and e-commerce platform dedicated to parrot care, focusing on detailed guides, responsible ownership, and providing high-quality products.

The core goal is to combine essential educational content (guides) for new and experienced parrot owners with a seamless shopping experience for food, toys, and accessories.
